About

Majid Mohammadi is a Vancouver-based fine-art photographer and pilot. His work blends disciplined observation with cinematic stillness—capturing the moment where light, weather, and terrain align.

Prints are available in gallery editions. Commissions and licensing by request.

How to photograph your space

  • Stand square-on to the wall; include floor and ceiling in frame.
  • Use natural light if possible; avoid strong shadows.
  • Place a common object (A4 paper, 30 cm ruler, or 12” LP) on the wall for scale.
  • Tell me approximate wall width and ceiling height for accurate sizing.
